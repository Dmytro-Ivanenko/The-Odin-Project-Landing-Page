Are You cat?
Find your breed here!

This website is made for people who identify themselves as cats — a playful space where you can discover which cat breed best matches your personality. Whether you're curious, cuddly, fierce, or independent, there's a purr-fect feline match waiting for you.

Very important information about ours breeds:

  • Gothic Cat — dark, elegant, and a bit mysterious. Enjoys silence, rainy nights, and music with deep emotions. Ideal for introverts with a gothic heart.

  • Cosmic Cat — a dreamy soul from another galaxy. Loves stargazing, deep thoughts, and floating in the universe of imagination. Perfect for those who believe in the magic of space.

  • Witch Cat — mystical and independent. Always knows what they want and seems to have a touch of magic. Your intuition will be your superpower with this breed.

  • Smart Cat — a true thinker. Loves puzzles, books, and planning ahead. If you're someone who enjoys deep thoughts and clever solutions, this is your feline match.

  • Rebellion Cat — bold, fearless, and never afraid to break the rules. This breed follows its own path, questions authority, and always stands out from the crowd. Perfect for the nonconformist spirit.
